About Us

Old Mutare United Methodist Youth Fellowship (UMYF) is a vibrant community of young people dedicated to serving God and growing in faith together. We welcome youth from all backgrounds to join us in worship, fellowship, and service activities.

Our mission is to provide a supportive environment where young people can explore their faith, develop leadership skills, and form lasting friendships grounded in Christian values.

We meet regularly for Bible study, prayer sessions, community service projects, and fun social events that help us grow closer as a community and deepen our relationship with Christ.
